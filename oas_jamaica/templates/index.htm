    Discover the many fascinating traces of our shared history and culture on this interactive site.

    Managed by the Jamaica National Heritage Trust, this interactive site represents an ongoing project of inventorying every place of heritage significance in the countryâ€”and documenting the people, events, and memories embodied in each.     The Barbados National Registry of Historic Places is the result of a cooperative initiative of the Jamaica Government and the Organization of American States and was funded by the U.S. Mission to the Organization of American States.

    The software powering the National Inventory is Arches, an open source heritage management system designed for built heritage by the Getty Institute and the World Monuments Fund.
